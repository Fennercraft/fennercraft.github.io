Community

Our primary method of communication and discussion is through the community forums. There players can ask questions, receive feedback, generate ideas, and suggest new features. The staff are constantly on the forums. The forums are helpful for our users with questions or concerns and for the staff when designing new games and fixing issues or modifying our current games. We encourage all of our users to register for the forums and help us by contributing to them in any way and as often as possible.
